Press Releases

  • High performance multitasking

    The MX6 is the first phone in our MX-series to use a 10-core A72-based CPU to guarantee high performance and processing speeds. All models have 4GB RAM, allowing users to switch between apps without losing any performance. The camera of the MX6 is powered by the Sony IMX386 CMOS sensor for exceptional image quality. The high-density phase detection pixel built into the sensor provides the MX6 with superior focus precision and speed.
